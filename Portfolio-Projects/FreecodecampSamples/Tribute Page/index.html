Lara Croft

The Tomb Raider

Quick Facts

  • First appearance: 1996 (Tomb Raider)
  • Origin: London, England
  • Occupation: Archaeologist, Adventurer
  • Known for: Daring expeditions and ingenuity

Short Timeline of Lara Croft

  • 1996 Tomb Raider Lara’s debut redefined 3D action‑adventure games.
  • 1997 Tomb Raider II Introduced vehicles and larger, more varied locations.
  • 1998 Tomb Raider III Non‑linear level paths and expanded traversal.
  • 1999 The Last Revelation A darker story with Egypt‑focused exploration.
  • 2000 Chronicles Anthology of Lara’s past adventures.
  • 2003 The Angel of Darkness A bold, experimental entry with RPG elements.

The initial Lara Croft is portrayed as an explorer with a take‑no‑prisoners attitude who pursued adventure for the thrill. Raised in British aristocracy, a plane crash and wilderness survival changed her path. Bored by upper‑class society, she funded expeditions through writing and became a gaming icon and a strong female lead.
